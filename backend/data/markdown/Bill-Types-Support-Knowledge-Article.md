**1\. Bill Type**


| Information |
| --- |
| If you are being charged the wrong amount it could be an incorrect bill type. In order to check your bill we will need you to check on bill type. Please do the following: 1\. Go to your student portal 2\. Select "make a payment" 3\. Bill type will be on the right\-hand corner As you fill out the following information please include your bill type. Create a ticket to the finance team. |






**2\. Direct Admit Changing to PC Bill Type**


| Information |
| --- |
| If you are already admitted to the Online Certificate and Degrees program (advanced path) but would like to complete the PathwayConnect program to receive the PathwayConnect rate you can do that. Once completed your billing account will need to be adjusted to receive the Pathway tuition rate. Make sure you complete an alternate assignment in PCB/PCIB 103 so you don't fill out an application. In addition, you need to have completed and met the following requirements:* PathwayConnect Debt * 3\.0 PathwayConnect Program GPA  If you have already completed PathwayConnect and matriculated please create a ticket for the finance team |
